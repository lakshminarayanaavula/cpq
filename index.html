Quick Start

  1. Enter your JWT token in the sidebar and click "Load All Data"
  2. Create Product Family to organize your catalog
  3. Add Product Line within each family
  4. Define Attributes for product configuration
  5. Create Products (Simple, Dynamic, or Bundle type)
  6. Add Supplier Address records under Supplier Info
  7. Manage Accounts, Quotes, Orders, and Assets under Sales
